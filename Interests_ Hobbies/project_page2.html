Information

Not bored enough? Visit the home section of any department store and you will. A mismatch of the beauty of home products and artifacts clash with rows lined with blisters -the metal hangers that hold products-. We designed this space to be functional, elegant and inspiring with recycled ebano wood in a grid system that embraces, not pushes you. Products are exhibited using one of two types of merchandisers: Pegboards that hold products vertically, turn them around to create a riser that create more stacking space without bulk and lush wood boxes. And not one blister.
